4. What are the purposes and lawful bases for processing personal data

Data processing is based on end-users’ consent.
We process your data to send the data download link and for statistical reasons.

5. What data do we process?

Paituli processes end user’s e-mail address.

6. Where do we get your data from?

Your data is acquired from you.

7. Where do we transfer your data?

We do not transfer personal data anywhere.

8. How do we protect your data and how long do we store it for?

Systems containing personal data can be accessed only by designated employees with their own access credentials.

End user’s e-mail address is processed for sending the e-mail with download link. For Paituli statistics pseudonymized e-mails are stored for maximum 2 years.

In case download e-mail could not be delivered (misspelled e-mail address, quota full etc), the download e-mail will forwarded to Paituli administrators. These e-mails are stored for user support for one month.

We regularly assess the need for storing data, considering the applicable legislation. We also take reasonable measures to ensure that the personal data is not contradictory to the data processing purposes, out of date or inaccurate. Where such data is identified, it is either corrected or destroyed without delay.

9. What are your rights as a data subject?

The following data subject rights are unconditional and in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to obtain information on the processing of personal data
  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Notification obligation regarding rectification or erasure of personal data or restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • The data subject can allow automated decision-making (including profiling) with his or her specific consent

If you suspect that your data is not processed according to the legislation, please contact up. You may also complain to the Data Protection Ombudsman.

10. Who should I contact?

All enquiries and requests regarding this privacy policy should be made in writing or in person to the contact person specified in section two (2).

11. Changes to privacy policy

Up-to-date Paituli privacy policy is available from Paituli service. The privacy service includes date of last update.
