Paituli

Paituli is a spatial data download service. Paituli's primary users are students and personnel of Finnish universities and polytechnics, but it is open to anyone. The service provides datasets that are important for research and education. Compared to other spatial data services Paituli includes also historical versions of datasets. For many datasets the first version is from 2005, when Paituli started. At the moment datasets from following data providers are available:

  • Finnish Digital and Population Data Services Agency
  • Finnish Food Agency
  • Finnish Meteorological Institute (FMI)
  • Finnish Transport Infrastructure Agency, Digiroad
  • Institute for the Languages of Finland (KOTUS)
  • Latuviitta
  • National Land Survey (MML)
  • Natural resource institute Finland (LUKE)
  • Statistics Finland
  • University of Helsinki, Digital Geography Lab

The detailed list of available datasets can be found on Metadata and Download data pages. Datasets have different open licenses, most datasets have CC-BY-4.0 license. Please remember to read the license terms before downloading the datasets.

Paituli provides four different options for downloading the data:

  • Datasets can be downloaded as zip-files from Download data page . The zip-file size is limited to 3Gb. Download data page provides also preview option for a big part of the datasets.
  • Bigger amounts of data can be download over HTTP, FTP and rsync .
  • Many datasets are also available as OGC web services: WMS (Maps), WCS, WMTS (Tiles) and WFS (Features).
  • CSC Puhti supercomputer has open GIS data , which includes a copy of almost all Paituli data, for fast and easy access for Puhti users.

Metadata of Paituli datasets is stored to Etsin . Metadata includes short description of dataset in Finnish and English, contact information, license terms and if available links to additional documents or web pages. Links to dataset specific pages in Etsin can be found on Metadata page or on Download page's Metadata tab.

Students and personnel of universities and polytechnics are welcomed to share data in Paituli.

The use of Paituli service is free of charge. Paituli is financed by Ministy of Education and Culture and maintained by CSC - IT center for science. Paituli is provided with best effort service level and availability.

Paituli is developed at CSC using open source software, inc: Geoserver, OpenLayers, PostGIS and Quarkus. Paituli source code is available in Github: front-end and back-end.
